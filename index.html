Chanse Campbell [Developer]

I first discovered programming completely by accident over four years ago whilst on a University study abroad programme. A new Facebook friend shared a video called "What most school's don't teach" and instantly I was hooked. Up until that point I hadn't been aware that getting into the world of tech was even a possibility for an English student. Discovering this was definitely a turning point that would define the start of a new career.

It took another three years to finally take the plunge and give up a promising job to dedicate my full attention to the craft of coding. After some self-taught learning, attending a coding bootcamp and working in several different developer roles, I'm a full time dev constantly looking for new things to learn and new ideas to build. Feel free to reach out to me if you'd like to chat about creating something together.
